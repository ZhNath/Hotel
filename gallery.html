Apartment Hause Casa Nostra

About this space This newly built and modernly furnished House (with 2 separate Apartments with each its own swimming pool) is located in the beautiful coastal town Rogoznica, between the bigger cities of Split and Sibenik. Perfect for big families to enjoy the quiet neighborhood outside on the green patio in the private swimming pool, with sun deck chairs and an outside sitting area. The space House consists of two separate apartments with each their own private entrance, as well as each with their own sun deck chairs and a swimming pool.

The First Apartment, that faces the street in front, has three sleeping rooms, two bathrooms and an open space living area with dining area and a fully equipped kitchen. The exit from the living room is on the patio with an outside dining table.

The Second Apartment, that faces the green backyard, has two sleeping rooms, two bathrooms and an open space living room with dining area and a fully equipped kitchen. The exit from the living room is on the little patio on the side with the green backyard, that has sun deck chairs and a swimming pool. The House has private parking place. Guest access The apartment is fully yours to enjoy!
